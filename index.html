Welcome to Herdsman Produce

Herdsman produce is a value addition company that strives to connect farmers to expanded markets that offer optimum value for their produce.
At Herdsman Produce we believe in the 'Trade Not Aid' model that allows farmers to participate wholly in actuallzing the fruits of their works .
We work diligently to expand the value chain by sourcing our products exclusively from small scale farmers while identifying different buyers and markets for the produce.

Our Products

We source our products exclusively from small-scale farmers in various regions of the country as we seek to build long-term localized capacity. Our products are distributed both locally & internationally.

Beef

Over the years, we have forged relationships with local farmers who rear the best breed of cattle so as to ensure we supply our vendors with top quality beef cuts ranging from fresh to chilled & frozen.

Goat & Mutton

We are in partnership with local farmers from the fertile pastures of ukambani & maasai land who supply us with naturally raised goats and sheep. It is these animals that we slaughter to produce quality meat for our clientele.

Chicken(Broilers)

We supply all sizes of broiler chicken to our vendors carefully sourced from a core group of farmers in our network at competitive prices. We also source for clean and carefully selected eggs for our clients.

Fresh vegetables

We supply a healthy variety of fresh & succulent vegetables all sourced from local farmers. We also supply carefully selected & precut potato chips(fries) prepared hygienically at our facilities.
